Trans Studio Mini Tegal

Tegal City, located in Central Java Province, Indonesia, is a city rich in history, culture and economic potential. Located on the north coast of Java, Tegal has a beautiful natural charm with towering white sand beaches and is famous for its spectacular sunsets. In addition, the city is also known as one of the important trade and industrial centers in Central Java. Tegal's history can be traced back to the Dutch colonial era, which has greatly influenced the city's architecture and culture. Several historical buildings such as Tegal Station and several old churches can still be found here. In addition, Tegal is also famous as a fisherman-friendly coastal city, with a busy harbor and a lively fish market. Travelers often visit this fish market to experience the authentic atmosphere and sample the delicious fresh seafood.
